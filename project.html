Cleveland Cavaliers 2021-2022 Mid-season review.

The Cleveland Cavaliers has been the league's suprise team for now, completely changing the team's image from being a bottom-feeder team. These changes are definetly something done overnight as they have been building their team with young talent all along these years. Along these roster changes include drafting dynamic guards/swingmen in Collin Sexton, Darius Garland and Isaac Okoro, trading for froncourt depth in Jarett Allen and Lauri Markkanen, and drafting a talented bigman in Evan Mobley. The sucess of this Cavaliers team shouldn't be overlooked, thus here are my mid season grades for each player based on their performance.
